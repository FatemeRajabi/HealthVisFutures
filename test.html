Welcome to HealthVisFutures Lab

Our research focuses on addressing real-world problems by designing effective technologies and information visualization tools. In particular, our research focuses on increasing patient engagement in their medical care, including supporting patients to track their health data, improving patient-provider communication, and assisting patients in making decisions about their health.
